HOW IT WORKS

From registration to transfusion — a streamlined 4-step journey.

1

Register & Verify

Create an account, choose your role, verify your identity, and complete your health profile.

2

Submit or Donate

Patients submit blood requests with urgency levels. Donors schedule appointments nearby.

3

Hospital Matches

The system auto-matches available blood units to requests based on type, urgency, and location.

4

Track & Confirm

Real-time status updates notify all parties. Admins review analytics and system health.
